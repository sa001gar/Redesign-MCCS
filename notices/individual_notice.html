Semester End Examination Schedule

The end semester examinations for all courses will commence from March 15, 2024 and continue until March 30, 2024. All students are required to check their exam centers and reporting times in the attached schedule document.

Important Guidelines:

  • Students must carry their valid University ID card to the examination center.
  • Mobile phones and electronic devices are strictly prohibited in the examination hall.
  • Students should arrive at the examination center at least 30 minutes before the scheduled start time.
  • Any student found using unfair means will be subject to disciplinary action as per University rules.
  • Students with pending fees will not be allowed to appear for the examinations without prior clearance from the accounts department.

Special Arrangements:
Students requiring special accommodations due to medical conditions or disabilities should contact the Examination Cell by March 5, 2024 with supporting documentation.

For any queries related to the examination schedule, please contact the Examination Cell at examcell@university.edu or visit Room 105, Academic Block during office hours (9:00 AM - 5:00 PM).
